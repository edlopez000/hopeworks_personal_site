Droning About

Communities

Reddit

While Reddit as a platform is not without its faults, it is home to the most openly available enthusiast communities and among them is a collection of FPV related subreddits. Anyone new to the hobby or merely interested can peruse the subreddits below and see how much the community seeks to help one another. As previously mentioned, the hobby is still in its wild-west phase where nothing is standardized and the learning curve is a bit steep. So any beginner will have to pick up skills that they previously might not have possessed like for example: soldering. The community is there to help in case you face some issue with some hyper-specific issue.

Resources

In many instances, the communities linked above can serve as the best resource in most cases. But in other situations, Joshua Bardwell is most hobbyists' next stop for anything FPV drone related. Bardwell runs a shopping list that helps beginners know what the best equipment in categories ranging from goggles to battery chargers. He also runs a youtube channel with hundreds of videos detailing the most basic to the most complex topics and concepts in FPV.
