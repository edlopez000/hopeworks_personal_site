Unless the pilot has previous experience, it can be difficult to have a successful first flight that doesnâ€™t begin and end with a wild drone flying around threatening everyone's safety. For this particular reason, it is recommended that you practice in a simulator beforehand. No simulator achieves a perfect simulacrum of real-world flight but they do help in teaching the mechanics of flying with gimbals. The one that is most recommended is Velocidrone. While the simulator can be played using many instruments, it is best to practice with a RC transmitter.
