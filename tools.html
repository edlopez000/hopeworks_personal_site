Droning About

Goggles

Without this tool, it wouldn’t be possible to fly the craft through first person view. Goggles come in a variety of shapes and sizes with each one facilitating different fidelity and video quality. Goggles come into two distinct camps when it comes to image quality: analog or digital. These two camps refer to the method that the video signal is transferred by. As in almost all instances, the digital signal is significantly greater. In the flying page, you will see an example of how it looks to fly through an analog and digital pair of goggles.

Drone

Drones are made up of many different components, among them: a flight controller, electronic speed controller, motors, camera, and video transmitter. The flight controller is the central processing unit for the drone and is responsible for understanding the drone’s position in 3D space and also for distributing the signals received from the transmitter to the electronic speed controllers. The ESCs are electronic components that regulate the electricity delivered to the motors. There are typically four motors that spin and propel the drone in any direction. Lastly the camera and video transmitter are responsible for capturing and distributing imagery.
